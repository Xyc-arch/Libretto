ID: TP-I-V-VI-IV
CATEGORY: progression
LEVEL: beginner

uplifting; the backbone of countless modern pop and rock songs.

CONCEPT: ii-V-I (jazz cadence)
ID: TP-II-V-I
CATEGORY: progression
LEVEL: intermediate
WHAT: The fundamental jazz cadence: ii-V-I. In C major: Dm-G-C. The ii sets up the V,
which resolves strongly to the I. Often played with sevenths (Dm7-G7-Cmaj7).
CHALLENGE: Play the three blocks and feel the strong root motion by descending fourths
into the tonic; the final bar repeats I to close.
