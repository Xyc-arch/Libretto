CONCEPT: Circle of fifths progression
ID: TP-CIRCLE-OF-FIFTHS
CATEGORY: progression
LEVEL: intermediate
WHAT: A diatonic circle-of-fifths sequence: the roots fall by fifths through every scale
degree back to the tonic. In C major: C-F-Bdim-Em-Am-Dm-G-C. Each chord's root is a fifth
below (or fourth above) the previous, the strongest possible root motion.
CHALLENGE: Play one block chord per bar and hear the relentless pull as each root drops a
fifth; note the diminished vii (Bdim) as the only diminished link in the chain.
